Tomato Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 quart tomato juice
  • salt to taste
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 (4 ounce) can artichoke hearts
  • 1 cup crumbled feta cheese

Steps:

  1. Medium heat the oven and saute onions in butter until translucent.
  2. Stir the flour so that no lumps remain, whisk tomato juice
  3. Add salt to taste, Let cool 10 minutes then slowly stir in milk
