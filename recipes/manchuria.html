Gobi Manchuria

Ingredients:

  • Manchurian Sauce
  • 5 tablespoons all-purpose-flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornmeal
  • 1/3 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 15 cauliflower florets

Steps:

  1. Mix flour and all the ingredients with cauliflower flourets
  2. Add manchuiran sauce inside the mix and also tomato sauce
  3. Fry the cauliflower flourets
  4. Garnish with green onions and serve
